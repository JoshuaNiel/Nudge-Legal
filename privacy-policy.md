## Data We Collect

### Account Information

When you create an account, We collect:

- Email address
- First name and last name
- Phone number (optional — used to receive SMS accountability messages from your friends)

### Screen Time Data

The core purpose of Nudge is screen time accountability. With your explicit permission, the Service collects:

- Daily app usage durations, broken down by app and category
- Goal configurations you set (app limits, category limits, daily totals)
- Goal evaluation results (met, breached, skipped)

This data is collected on-device using Apple's Screen Time framework and stored in your private account. It is not shared with other users or third parties.

### Friend Contact Information

When you add a friend for accountability, We collect:

- Your friend's name (as you enter it)
- Your friend's mobile phone number (in E.164 format, e.g. +1XXXXXXXXXX)

This information is used solely to send SMS consent and accountability messages to that friend via Twilio (our SMS Service Provider). We do not use friend phone numbers for any other purpose.

### SMS Consent and Messaging

Before sending any messages to a friend, We obtain their explicit consent via SMS. Friends may withdraw consent at any time by replying **STOP**.

We retain the following SMS-related records:

- Whether a friend has consented, declined, or opted out
- Timestamps and content of accountability messages sent
- Friend replies (e.g. encouragement or check-in responses)

### Device Tokens

If you enable push notifications, We collect your device's APNs token to deliver friend replies to your device. Tokens are stored securely and used only for that purpose.

### Usage and Diagnostic Data

We collect limited technical data to maintain and improve the Service:

- App crash reports and error logs (no personally identifiable information)
- General feature usage patterns (aggregated and anonymized)

We do **not** collect IP addresses, browser data, or precise location information.

## How We Use Your Data

We use Personal Data only for the following purposes:

- **To provide the Service** — delivering core features: screen time tracking, goal management, friend accountability, and SMS messaging.
- **To maintain your account** — managing authentication and profile information.
- **To send accountability messages** — forwarding nudges and reports to friends who have consented to receive them.
- **To deliver friend replies** — sending push notifications to you when a friend responds to a nudge.
- **To improve the Service** — analyzing aggregated, anonymized usage patterns to identify improvements.

We do **not** use your Personal Data for marketing communications, advertising, or promotional purposes.

---

## How We Share Your Data

We share Personal Data only in the following limited circumstances:

- **With Service Providers** — We share phone numbers with Twilio solely to send and receive SMS messages on your behalf. Twilio is bound by its own privacy policy and data processing terms and is not permitted to use your data for any other purpose.
- **Legal compliance** — We may disclose Personal Data if required by law, court order, or government authority, or to protect the rights and safety of our users.
- **Account deletion** — Upon account deletion, Personal Data is removed from our systems in accordance with the retention schedule below.

We do **not** sell your Personal Data. We do **not** share your Personal Data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. We do **not** share your Personal Data with affiliates, business partners, or other users beyond what is described above.

## Data Retention

| Data Category | Retention Period |
|---|---|
| Account information (name, email) | Duration of account + up to 24 months after closure |
| Screen time and goal data | Duration of account + up to 24 months after closure |
| Friend contact information | Until you delete the contact or your account |
| SMS message records | Up to 24 months |
| Device tokens | Until you disable notifications or delete your account |
| Crash logs and diagnostics | Up to 12 months |

When retention periods expire, Personal Data is securely deleted or irreversibly anonymized. Residual copies may remain in encrypted backups for a limited period and are not actively processed.

You may request early deletion of your data at any time by contacting Us or deleting your account through the app.
